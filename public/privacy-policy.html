Scrap Frontier Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-21

Overview

Scrap Frontier stores gameplay progress on the local device and may use platform services for rewarded ads, in-app purchases, and crash or billing diagnostics when the mobile build is enabled.

Data Used By The Game

  • Local save data such as base progress, roster, research, and shop entitlements.
  • Ad and purchase state needed to unlock rewards or restore purchases.
  • Basic device or platform diagnostics returned by the mobile commerce plugin.

How Data Is Stored

The web build stores progress in local browser storage. Mobile builds may also rely on platform billing or ad SDKs to validate purchases and rewarded views.

Third-Party Services

Mobile builds can integrate third-party ad and billing SDKs through Capacitor. Their data handling is governed by the privacy policies of the services chosen for the final release.
